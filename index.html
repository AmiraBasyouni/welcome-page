composition

One day, I dream of publishing my own music! A challenge I've been facing is digitalizing my work. Often, when I try to recreate my music on an application, it doesn't turn out the way I imagined it. To overcome this, I'd like to get better at reading and writing sheet music. I have many musical ideas but I struggle to document them.

inspiration

An artist I'm currently inspired by is Mishaal Tamer, he's from Saudi Arabia. It's amazing when there is an artist of the same culture as you. He fasts for ramadan, speaks Arabic, and shares the same values. I tend to come up with songs when I'm expressing thoughts and emotions. I think he follows a similar process to that.
